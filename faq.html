Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before planning your journey with us

What is included in your tour packages? ▾

Our tour packages typically include transportation, hotel accommodation, guided sightseeing, and selected meals. Exact inclusions are clearly mentioned in each package description.

Are meals and accommodation included in the price? ▾

Yes, accommodation and daily breakfast are included in most packages. Dinner is also included for selected destinations. Any exclusions are transparently listed.

Is the tour suitable for families and elderly travelers? ▾

Absolutely. We design our itineraries to be comfortable and flexible. Families and senior travelers are welcome, and we can adjust activities based on specific needs.

What should I pack for northern areas like Hunza or Kashmir? ▾

We recommend warm clothing, comfortable walking shoes, personal medication, and weather protection. A reminder checklist is shared with all confirmed travelers.

How do you handle bad weather or road closures? ▾

Safety is our priority. In case of weather or road issues, itineraries are adjusted or alternative attractions are offered. Our team remains in constant contact with local authorities.

Do you offer customized or private tours? ▾

Yes, we offer fully customized and private tour options. You can contact us with your preferences, and our team will design a personalized itinerary.
