Azad Kashmir Tour

5 Days | Lakes & Valleys | Scenic Adventure

Package Overview

Discover the serene beauty of Azaad Kashmir with Dream Travels. Enjoy crystal-clear lakes, lush valleys, cultural highlights, and comfortable travel throughout the region.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 01 – Arrival in Muzaffarabad

Morning departure to Muzaffarabad. Check-in at hotel, brief city tour, enjoy local cuisine, and evening rest.

Day 02 – Neelum Valley Excursion

Explore the beautiful Neelum Valley, visit Keran, Sharda, and scenic viewpoints along the river.

Day 03 – Ratti Gali Lake & Surroundings

Jeep and hiking excursion to Ratti Gali Lake. Enjoy alpine views and photography. Return to Neelum Valley for overnight stay.

Day 04 – Shounter Valley & Arang Kel

Visit the picturesque Shounter Valley and Arang Kel hilltop village. Experience local culture and scenic walks.

Day 05 – Return to Islamabad

Breakfast and checkout. Journey back to Islamabad with scenic stops along the way. Arrival by evening.
