Naran Valley Tour

5 Days | Scenic Valleys | Alpine Lakes

Package Overview

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Naran Valley with Dream Travels. This carefully planned tour includes comfortable transport, hotel stays, guided sightseeing, and unforgettable natural landscapes.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 01 – Departure & Arrival in Naran

Early morning departure from Islamabad via Hazara Motorway. Short refreshment stops along the way. Arrival in Naran by evening. Hotel check-in, rest, and dinner.

Day 02 – Saif-ul-Malook Lake Excursion

After breakfast, jeep excursion to Saif-ul-Malook Lake. Free time for sightseeing and photography. Return to Naran, visit local bazaars. Overnight stay.

Day 03 – Lalazar & Upper Kaghan Valley

Morning drive to Lalazar for stunning meadows. Explore Upper Kaghan Valley, visit Shogran viewpoint. Evening return to hotel. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 04 – Babusar Top & Surrounding Valleys

After breakfast, visit Babusar Top. Enjoy panoramic views and photo opportunities. Explore nearby valleys and streams. Return to Naran for overnight stay.

Day 05 – Return Journey

Breakfast and hotel checkout. Return journey to Islamabad with scenic stops. Arrival by night.
