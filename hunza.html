Hunza Valley Tour

5 Days | Majestic Peaks | Cultural Experience

Package Overview

Explore the breathtaking Hunza Valley with Dream Travels. Experience the grandeur of Rakaposhi and Ultar peaks, visit ancient forts, and immerse yourself in local culture while enjoying comfortable travel.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 01 – Arrival in Gilgit

Depart early from Islamabad. Stop for refreshments and scenic photos. Arrive in Gilgit, check-in, rest, and explore the city.

Day 02 – Hunza Valley Exploration

Drive to Hunza Valley. Visit Altit & Baltit Forts, Karimabad viewpoint, and local bazaars. Overnight stay in Hunza.

Day 03 – Passu & Khunjerab Pass

Full-day excursion to Passu Glacier and Khunjerab Pass. Return to Hunza for overnight stay.

Day 04 – Hopper Glacier & Attabad Lake

Visit Hopper Glacier and take a boat ride at Attabad Lake. Enjoy the stunning views and photography opportunities.

Day 05 – Return Journey

Breakfast and checkout. Return trip to Islamabad with scenic stops. Arrival by evening.
